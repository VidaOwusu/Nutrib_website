About Us NUTRIBLEND

We are an agritech processing company located in Sunyani-Fiapre, specializing in the production of high-quality, non-dairy milk alternatives made from tiger nuts and cashews. Our products are available in both powdered and liquid forms. Established in 2023, our mission is to enhance the availability and variety of nutritious, lactose-free options for individuals with lactose intolerance or those seeking healthier dairy substitutes..
